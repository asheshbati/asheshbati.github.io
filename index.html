Maths

I'm interested in sheaves, bundles on curves, and stability. In particular, I like to work with their moduli spaces, and I often think using the derived category. In general I work in Algebraic Geometry.

Right now, I'm looking at curves on K3 surfaces, and how this condition affects certain Brill-Noether loci on these curves, as well as how bundles on these curves affect sheaves on the K3 surface.

Previously, during my masters' I primarily looked at enumerative invariants, in particular the GW/DT correspondence and its proof in the toric case.
